Lasagna

An easy and tasty italian recipe

This is an easy to follow recipe where you don't need any equipment, but it would sure would not hurt to have a mixer or a large counter to be able to handle the mix better

We will follow the traditional way as taught by many italians where we use flour, eggs, oil and a little bit of water to do the dough and simple ham and cheese for the filling

Ingredients

  • 500g of flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 10 mg of salt
  • 500g of ricota cheese
  • 300g of ham

Steps

  1. mix the flour, eggs, oil and salt together until you get a consistent, firm and not sticky dough
  2. make see through sheets with the dough
  3. One by one put the sheets in boiling water until they get a white-ish colour, then put them in cold water and dry them
  4. Put the sheets on a tray with the filling in between
  5. Put it in the oven for aroung 45min at 180 degrees celsius
