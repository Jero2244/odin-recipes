Lasagna

An easy and tasty italian recipe

This is an easy to follow recipe where you don't need any equipment, but it would sure would not hurt to have a mixer or a large counter to be able to handle the mix better

We will follow the traditional way as taught by many italians where we use flour, eggs, oil and a little bit of water to do the dough and simple ham and cheese for the filling

Ingredients

  • 500g of flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 10 mg of salt
